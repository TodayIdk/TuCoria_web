Username Rules

Your username is your identity in TuCoria. To keep the community safe and friendly, we enforce the following rules using both automated AI checks and human review.

Format

  • Length: 4 to 32 characters
  • Allowed: Latin letters A–Z, digits 0–9, underscore _, dash -, dot .
  • Not allowed: spaces, cyrillic, emoji, special symbols
  • Case-insensitive: Vlad and vlad are the same

Prohibited content

The following will result in an automatic ban by our AI moderator:

  • Profanity, swears, insults (any language)
  • Sexual / NSFW content, references to sex, body parts
  • Slurs (racial, homophobic, transphobic, religious, etc.)
  • Nazism, fascism, extremism, terrorism references
  • Numeric hate codes: 1488, 88, KKK
  • Violence: kill, rape, murder, suicide, "kys"
  • Drugs and drug slang
  • Child exploitation content of any kind
  • Impersonation of staff, admins, developers, or the owners Today_Idk and KAMKIN
  • Spam: URLs, phone numbers, promo codes, social media handles
  • Obfuscated attempts to bypass filters (e.g. f_u_c_k, sh1t, homoglyphs)

What happens if my name is flagged?

  1. Your account is restricted — you can't access the game or profile.
  2. You'll see a ban page explaining the reason.
  3. You can appeal by changing your username to something compliant.
  4. Once you pick a new valid username, your account is restored instantly.

Reserved names

The following names cannot be used to prevent confusion or impersonation:

  • admin, moderator, staff, support, owner, creator, developer
  • system, bot, server, root, official
  • tucoria, today_idk, kamkin and variations
